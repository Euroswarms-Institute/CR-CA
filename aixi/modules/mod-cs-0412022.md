# Module hook: cs/0412022 (Zeno machines and hypercomputation)

**URL:** https://arxiv.org/pdf/cs/0412022

**Bibliographic note:** Petrus H. Potgieter, *Zeno machines and hypercomputation* (arXiv:cs/0412022v3). Survey/review of Church–Turing-style theses, models of “hypercomputation,” and **Zeno machines** (Turing machines equipped with an accelerating clock so that infinitely many steps may occur in finite physical time, in the idealized model). Emphasizes that halting-type limitations accompany reasonable computational models and cautions against overstated claims of having “broken the Turing barrier,” while defending the continued foundational role of Turing computability.
